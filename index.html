I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Political and Social Sciences at the European University Institute. I was a visitng scholar at the Department of Political Science at the University of Mannheim (March-April 2025) and at the Bren School of Environmental Science and Management at the University of California, Santa Barbara (Autumn 2023).

I research the politics of climate change, with my dissertation work focussed on causes of changes in public opinion and political behaviour related to climate change. I am a founding member of the EUI Environmental and Climate Politics Working Group and am actively engaged with the Environmental Politics and Governance Network.

I hold a BA in European Studies from Trinity College Dublin and an MA in EU International Relations and Diplomacy Studies from the College of Europe in Bruges.
