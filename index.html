My current research is on the political impacts of climate-related natural disasters. My broader interests are in environmental and climate change politics, European and African politics. I am a founding member of the EUI Environmental and Climate Politics Working Group and am also actively engaged with the Harvard Climate Pipeline Project and CEGA Working Group on African Political Economy.

I hold a BA in European Studies from Trinity College Dublin and an MA in EU International Relations and Diplomacy Studies from the College of Europe in Bruges.
